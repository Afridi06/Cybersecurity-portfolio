# A10: Discover Privacy Techniques Used Offline

## Overview
This activity explores privacy protection techniques used in offline (non-digital) environments.


## Privacy Techniques Identified

### 1. Locked Mailbox
- **Description:** Mail is delivered into a locked mailbox
- **Purpose:** Prevent unauthorized access to personal information
- **Privacy Concept:** Data Protection + Confidentiality

## Reflection
Offline privacy techniques play a critical role in protecting sensitive information from physical access and observation. Simple practices such as shredding documents and hiding PINs demonstrate how human behaviour contributes to maintaining privacy.



## Conclusion
Privacy is not limited to digital systems. Offline techniques are essential in preventing information leakage and protecting personal data in everyday environments.
