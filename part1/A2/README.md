## Reflection
Public spaces rely heavily on visible security measures to deter crime and ensure safety. These systems focus on monitoring, enforcement and rapid response.
Many security mechanisms such as CCTV cameras are intentionally placed in visible locations to make individuals aware that they are being monitored. This acts as a deterrent, reducing the likelihood of theft or malicious behaviour.



## Conclusion
Security in public spaces combines surveillance, law enforcement, and preventive technologies to protect people and property.
