# A24: Teach Your Family About a Cybersecurity Topic

## Overview
This activity involves teaching a cybersecurity topic to family members to improve their awareness and understanding of online safety.

## Topic Taught: Phishing Attacks

### Explanation
- I explained what phishing is and how attackers try to trick users into giving personal information
- Showed examples of fake emails and messages that look legitimate
- Explained how phishing links can lead to fake websites


## Key Points Taught

### 1. How to Identify Suspicious Emails
- Check sender email address carefully
- Look for spelling mistakes and unusual requests
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Security Concept: Social Engineering Awareness
